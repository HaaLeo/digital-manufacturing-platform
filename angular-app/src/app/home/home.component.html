3D Printer - Use case

A Blockchain application using Hyperledger Composer and BigchainDB for buying and printing 3D Printer models.

Designers upload their original models and set the price for each model. These models are avaliable to the end-users for 3D printing. The end-users select which models they want to print and pay the cost. The designers upload a copy of the already bought models and the printers print the 3D objects and transfer the money to the corresponding model creators.
